Places Must Visit

Grand Canyon National Park

I had visited in March 6 2022

Grand Canyon is a very good place to travel for vacation! Wonderful views and nice trails. If you just randomly take a photo, it will look good. At night, you can see thousand of stars so clearly! Its to ba that the phone cannot see the stars, otherwise I would have taken photos of them. The only downside is the hotel. The wifi is very bad and it is very loud during the night because of the mini fridge. Even when we remove the mini fridge's plug, it is still loud for some reason. But fortunately, it is worth it because you can even see so many mule deer on your way out!



White Sand National Park

I had visited in March 2 2022

This place is so fun! It is so fun so slide down the high dunes over and over again! The only exeption is that is hot and you have the buy a sled and wax. Fortunately, the sled and wax are not that expensive.
